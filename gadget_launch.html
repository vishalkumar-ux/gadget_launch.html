Vision VR Headset

The Vision VR is a new virtual reality headset for gaming and entertainment.

A VR headset, or virtual reality headset, is a head-mounted device designed to immerse the user in a virtual environment. It typically features 3D near-eye displays, positional tracking, and may come in various forms such as goggles or helmets. These devices create an immersive experience by using stereoscopic 3D lenses and screens inside the headset, allowing users to interact with computer-generated environments. Some VR headsets are self-contained, meaning they do not require external hardware to operate
